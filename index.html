About CYBER BRIEF

CYBER BRIEF creates short cybersecurity news updates and educational videos designed to explain security issues in clear, accessible language. Our content covers newly disclosed software vulnerabilities, real-world security incidents, online scams, and practical security awareness topics for a general audience.

All videos are original productions created and owned by CYBER BRIEF. AI-assisted tools may be used during content production.

What We Publish

Security News Shorts

Short vertical videos explaining newly disclosed vulnerabilities and security incidents — what happened, who is affected, and why it matters to everyday users.

Security Education

Longer explainer videos covering security fundamentals: phishing and smishing, password safety, multi-factor authentication, privacy myths, and how common scams work.

Weekly Digest

A weekly roundup of the most significant security stories, summarized for a general audience.

CYBER BRIEF Publisher

CYBER BRIEF Publisher is a publishing application operated by CYBER BRIEF. It is used to distribute original CYBER BRIEF content to social media accounts owned and operated by CYBER BRIEF, through the official APIs of supported platforms (such as TikTok and YouTube).

The application is used exclusively for publishing content created and owned by CYBER BRIEF. It has no public user accounts and does not collect information about viewers or followers. See the Privacy Policy and Terms of Service for details.
