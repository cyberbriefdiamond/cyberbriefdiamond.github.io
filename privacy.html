Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how CYBER BRIEF Publisher ("the Application") handles information. The Application is operated by CYBER BRIEF and is used to publish original CYBER BRIEF cybersecurity videos to social media accounts owned and operated by CYBER BRIEF.

Information Accessed

The Application accesses only the information required to publish content:

  • Basic account information obtained through platform authorization.
  • Publishing permissions granted through official platform APIs.
  • Video files, titles, captions and metadata selected for publication.

Information We Do Not Collect

  • No public user accounts.
  • No viewer information.
  • No follower information.
  • No advertising identifiers.
  • No analytics or behavioral tracking technologies.
  • No website cookies.

OAuth and Platform Access

The Application uses the official authorization mechanisms provided by supported platforms. Access is limited to accounts controlled by CYBER BRIEF. The Application does not access, collect or process data belonging to any other person or account.

TikTok Platform Data

When connected to TikTok, the Application uses TikTok's official APIs to access basic account information about the connected CYBER BRIEF account (such as display name and avatar) and to upload and publish videos to that account.

  • Data obtained from TikTok is used solely to publish CYBER BRIEF content to the connected CYBER BRIEF account.
  • Data obtained from TikTok is not sold, shared, or used for advertising, analytics, profiling, or any other purpose.
  • Only authentication tokens are retained, and only while the TikTok integration remains active.
  • Access can be revoked at any time from the connected TikTok account's settings (Settings and privacy → Security & permissions → Apps and services), which invalidates the Application's access.

YouTube API Services

When connected to YouTube, the Application uses YouTube API Services to upload and manage videos on the CYBER BRIEF channel. Use of YouTube API Services is subject to the YouTube Terms of Service and the Google Privacy Policy. Granted access can be revoked at any time via Google security settings.

Data Storage

Authentication credentials and access tokens are stored on infrastructure controlled by the operator of CYBER BRIEF and are protected against unauthorized access. Access credentials may be revoked at any time through the corresponding platform settings.

Data Sharing

Information is not sold, rented, licensed or disclosed to third parties. Content is transmitted only to the platform selected for publication, using that platform's official API.

Data Retention and Deletion

Credentials and access tokens are retained only while publishing integrations remain active. Stored credentials and tokens are deleted when an integration is disconnected, or upon request. Deletion requests may be sent to cyberbriefdiamond@gmail.com and are honored promptly.

Children's Privacy

The Application is a business publishing tool and is not directed at children. It does not knowingly collect information from anyone, including children.

Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. The most recent version will remain available at this address.

Contact

Questions regarding this Privacy Policy may be sent to: cyberbriefdiamond@gmail.com
